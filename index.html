What's so special about Ideazy?

Ideazy is a unique way to find new and exciting app ideas, and to talk with the person who made the idea. Ideazy skips all posts that fit any of the following criteria:
  • Dating app
  • People asking for feedback on existing apps
  • Is already built (download links, "i built" found in text, asking for testers, etc)
  • Omegle alternatives (we don't need another one...)
  • Asking for something (eg app ideas)
  • People looking for a dev (not the best info)
  • Posts with polls (these are more or less "expreimental" so to speak)
